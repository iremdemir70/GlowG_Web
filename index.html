Problem definition:

Skin care is vital for both physical health and aesthetics, but identifying skin type and selecting appropriate products can be challenging. Misjudging skin type often leads to using incorrect products, causing issues like irritation, dryness, oiliness, or acne. This not only harms skin health but also results in financial losses and reduced confidence in skincare. The wide range of products and lack of knowledge about ingredients further complicates the decision-making process, forcing individuals to rely on advertisements and generic advice.

Problem solution:

GlowGenie is an AI-powered skincare platform that simplifies access to accurate and reliable solutions. It helps users identify their skin type through simple questions and analyzes product ingredients for compatibility. The platform also provides personalized product recommendations based on users' skin types and preferences. By offering a fast, reliable, and tailored approach, GlowGenie enhances the skincare experience, empowering users to make informed decisions for healthier and more effective skincare.
